Expression Syntax

AB' + AC — AND by adjacency, OR by +, NOT by apostrophe !A + B — ! also works as NOT A^B — XOR (also accepts ⊕) A'B + BC' + A^C — mixed example (A^B)C' — parentheses supported
Variables: A, B, C, D  ·  ^ or ⊕ = XOR  ·  ' or ! = NOT  ·  + = OR  ·  adjacency = AND

Cell Colours

Blue — output is 1, not covered by expression
Green — output is 1 and expression agrees
Amber — expression covers this cell but output is 0 (over-coverage)

Group of 1 — full minterm

Group of 2 — one variable dropped

Group of 4 — two variables dropped

Group of 8 — three variables dropped

Toroidal wrapping: leftmost/rightmost columns are logically adjacent (Gray code), as are top and bottom rows. Corner groups of 4 are valid.
