Below is a transistor switch, which for our purposes will become our first logic gate.

You can interact with it as follows:

  • Click the input to toggle it low (brown) or high (red).
  • Click the output to toggle it low (brown) or high (blue).
  • Click the transistor to toggle it as cutoff (brown) or saturated (green).

When you think you have a consistent state, hit check circuit.
